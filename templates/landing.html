How CPIE protects you

A three-tier inspection pipeline designed around privacy and accuracy.

1

Sender Trust Evaluation

Checks SPF, DKIM, DMARC, domain age, and communication history to generate a Sender Trust Score — without reading your email body.

2

Deep Content Analysis

Only for suspicious senders — NLP detects urgency and fear tactics, OCR scans embedded images, and the link analyzer checks every URL.

3

Cognitive Protection Layer

Risky emails show an annotated view with highlighted threats and explanations. High-risk links open in a safe sandbox environment.
